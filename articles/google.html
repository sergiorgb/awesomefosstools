Google: embrace, extend, and extinguish

Google, a multi-billon dollar company valued at 1.32 Trillon dollars is just a humongous company with a wide variety of services. That's what we all know but have you ever asked yourself, how does Google make money?, if you didn't think about that before i've got the answer for you Tracking you for selling your data for advertising purposes. 60% of the ads that you see in websites, blogs and social media are handled by Google’s AdSense technology, they’ve used the power of having the all mighty Google Chrome to track user data in every step of the process as well as buying out competitors to expand their power. Google’s services go from education to enterprise cloud services, Everyone you know probably uses one of those services. Google from a business perspective is a great investment, but that investment goes into new technology to track more and more people generating more money than ever before.
Google won’t ever stop doing this unless you don’t use their services you can opt into selfhosted services, or paid services that respect you and your privacy. No government has the power to stop these ( the EU could save us though ) and users don’t care about that, yet everyone uses their services.
I’d want to stop using such services, but google vendor-locks you into them, mostly due to my school and my gmail account, if you ever try to leave google for good, I wish you luck and I hope you can stop google from using you.
